How It Works

Eden works by giving you a well-trained home concierge (we call them Gardeners), who handles your home’s chores while you go about your day.

  • 1You tell us about your home needs.
  • 2We assign you an Eden Gardener.
  • 3Your Gardener takes over all your home's tasks.
  • 4You monitor the status of the chores on your app and leave feedback where necessary.
